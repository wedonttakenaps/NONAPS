Irresistibility Collages

These quick collages are fantastical diagrams of irresistibility. Though we couldn't put a name to it until these studies, irresitibility is an essential component to our work. We want to create inhabitable spaces, installations, and art that people are drawn to, that they are compelled to explore, even if they don't totally understand what they're looking at or why they're attracted to it. It's something we will continue study and incorporate into every NONAPS project.
